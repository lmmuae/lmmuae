GRENA

Grena Ltd is an innovative manufacturer of disposable medical devices.

Grena's' focus is on patients’ safety and users’ comfort we offer cost-effective quality products. Their range consists of stapling instruments (including circular staplers, linear staplers and skin staplers), ligating clips, surgical meshes,endoscopic instruments and consumable medical products for general surgery like thoracentesis/paracentesis sets, connecting tubes, insuflaction needles, retrieval bags, suction sets and chest drainage systems.

Grena maintains their certified quality management system based on EN ISO13485:2003 that helps them serve clients better. They are also proud to have TÜV Rheinland, Germany as their Notified Body.

PROSAN

Prosan International B.V is a manufacturer of the hormone free Flexi-T Intra-Uterine Devices.

Prosan's Flexi-T IUDs are optimally safe and have effective copper IUDs for nulliparous and multiparous women. The development of the various Flexi-T types was based on extensive in vivo longitudinal and transversal measurements of uterine cavities of all ages and parity groups.

With the fundus seeking design, its high flexibility and the easy insertion system Flexi-T has proven to be the optimal IUD for millions of women.

REGULAT

With its innovative, premium-quality products, Dr. Niedermaier Pharma GmbH's Regulat is making important contributions to improving health and enhancing quality of life.

Regulatpro® is a cascade-fermented enzyme drink made from organically grown fruit, nuts, vegetables and spices. The bioactive substances from the valuable source materials are broken down by the patented cascade fermentation and made highly available in a concentrated form inside Regulatpro®.

The enzymatic regulation efficacy of Regulat® has a wide variety of influence on the entire body as well as a local effect on and through the skin.

X1R

Founded in 1989 as “Sun Coast Chemicals”, The X-1R Corporation originally pioneered the motor sport/racing industry with superior technology. Since that time, X-1R has maintained the same high standards with our broadened product line of lubricants, greases, fuel detergents and racing additives.

X-1R’s concentrated lubricant is used to eliminate engine friction. Other products utilizing this amazing technology include a full line of greases, air conditioner compressor fluids, gear and hydraulic fluids and penetrating spray. X-1R sponsors cars in every motorsport level, including NASCAR, bearing the “X-1R Friction Eliminator” logo.

X-1R is the only lubricant company to be inducted into the Space Foundation/NASA Space Technology Hall of Fame; no other lubricant technology than X-1R is exclusively accredited as, “Certified Space Technology”.
